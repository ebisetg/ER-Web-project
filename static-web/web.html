Events

Football Tournaments

Weekly matches teaching teamwork, discipline, and confidence. Safe spaces where street children learn leadership through sports.

Quarterly showcase of children's artwork. Creative expression helping heal trauma and build self-worth.

Monthly educational visits to museums, parks and farms. Broadening horizons beyond street life.

Seasonal events (Christmas, Eid, Meskel) creating joyful memories. Every child deserves celebration.
